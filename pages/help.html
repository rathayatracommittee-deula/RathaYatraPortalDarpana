How To Download Photos ?

Open the photos section from the home page or navigation bar and select the year section from which you want the photos and click any photo from the photos-gallery and click the Download Photo button to download the photo.

Where To Watch Videos And Download ?

Open the Videos section from the home page or navigation bar and select the respective year from which you want to download the videos and select the events and click watch or download button of any video that you want.

How To Watch Live ?

Click the Live section on the home page and click the Watch Live button to watch the Live, if Live is ended then subscribe to our official youtube channel and turn on bell icon to get the Live notification immediately when we we will go Live.

How To Contact The Committee ?

Open the Contact Section on the home page and select the option through which you want to contact- call, WhatsApp, email, or view locations.

How To Find The Places ?

Use the Location section or in Contact section there are the locations of Mandir , Mausi Badi and Ratha Yatra Field. Click the open location button to get the locations through google maps.

If Found a problem ?

If found any mistakes on website or found any mistakes in photos , videos or in other section of this site then Please contact the committee through the Contact page and report the issue.
